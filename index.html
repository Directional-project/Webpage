About
Directional
Directional is a blockchain-based decentralized peer-to-peer exchange platform which allows both retail and institutional investors to participate in securities finance trading. If you have a security you wish to lend in return for fair market return, enter into a lending contract in Directional, or should you wish to borrow stocks for short-selling, participate in Directional as a borrower.
